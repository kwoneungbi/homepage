ONGIC has been a leading petrochemical trader and general contractor based in Seoul, Korea. Established in 1992 as a part of DAU group (a petrochemical group with five affiliate companies in the oil and gas industry), we have successfully participated in numerous gas and oil projects for many prestigious clients in different parts of the world.

 

From preparing feasibility study of upstream projects to marketing processed products, we involve in every step of the value-chain of the petrochemical industry. Our commitment to execute projects with highest quality, on schedule and within budget while adhering to excellent standards, we are recognized as the complete petrochemical solution provider.
